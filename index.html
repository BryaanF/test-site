What is Notion

Notion is note taking apps, besides note taking you can save anything related to the text or media based. Just like a blog but personal to you, Notion is not far different from that. Inside of you can save some picture or text that maybe you need it for later.

In the other hand you can make notes for your client or makes anything that you can think of. See Notion as multi functional app around notes and you can do anything about that notes, whether you want it to be keeping for yourself or share it with another people. Besides you can add some collaborator to your writing so they can edit it and you can work together make the work done faster.
